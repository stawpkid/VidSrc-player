How to Find and Copy Your Movie's IMDb ID

  1. Visit IMDb.com and search for your movie.
  2. Once you've found the movie, look at the URL in your browser.
  3. The movie's IMDb ID will be a string starting with "tt" followed by a series of numbers (e.g., tt1234567).
  4. Copy that IMDb ID. You will use this ID for embedding, referencing, or any further actions.
